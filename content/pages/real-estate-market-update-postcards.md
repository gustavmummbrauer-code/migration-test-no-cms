Market Update Postcards are an effective way for real estate agents and REALTORS to provide valuable insights to their sphere of influence and farm areas—keeping homeowners informed about local real estate trends.

These postcards feature up-to-date market statistics, including average home prices, recent sales, and days on market in specific neighborhoods. By sharing timely market data, you position yourself as a knowledgeable local expert who provides relevant information that homeowners appreciate.

Real estate market update postcards can also include a call to action, encouraging homeowners to contact you for a personalized home valuation or to scan a QR code for an automated property report. This not only offers value but also opens the door for new client interactions.

The real estate industry recognizes a clear trend: agents who frequently send out market update postcards often secure more listings.
